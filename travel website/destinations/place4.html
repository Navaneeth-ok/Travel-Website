Welcome to Karnataka

Karnataka, known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes, is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. From historical monuments to vibrant cities, there's something for every traveler.

Attractions

  • Hampi: Explore the ruins of the ancient city of Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Mysore Palace: Visit the stunning Mysore Palace, known for its architectural beauty and historical significance.
  • Coorg: Enjoy the lush greenery and coffee plantations in the picturesque hill station of Coorg.

Travel Tips

Don't miss out on trying the local cuisine, especially the dosas and filter coffee! Always carry a light jacket as the weather can be unpredictable.
