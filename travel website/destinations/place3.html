Welcome to Kashmir

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth", is known for its breathtaking landscapes, gardens, and rich culture.

Attractions

  • Srinagar: Experience the beauty of Dal Lake and its houseboats.
  • Gulmarg: Enjoy skiing and the stunning views of the Himalayas.
  • Pahalga: Explore the lush green meadows and picturesque landscapes.

Travel Tips

Plan your trip during the summer months for the best experience.
