Welcome to Kerala

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is famous for its lush green landscapes, tranquil backwaters, and rich cultural heritage. It's a paradise for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Attractions

  • Backwaters: Experience the serene beauty of the backwaters in Alappuzha.
  • Hill Stations: Explore the scenic hill stations like Munnar and Wayanad.
  • Wildlife Sanctuaries: Discover diverse wildlife in Thekkady and Periyar National Park.

Travel Tips

Make sure to explore the local markets and try the street food! Also, don't forget to carry sunscreen and insect repellent for your outdoor adventures.
